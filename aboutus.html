About Our Expertise

Building trust through scientific precision since 1969.

55+ Years of Excellence

Established in the year 1969, Ana Laboratories is ISO 9001:2008 certified service provider engaged in offering analysis of various products in the field of environments, food products, agriculture, geo-chemistry, chemicals, metallurgy, pesticides, oils, pharmaceuticals & cosmetics and herbs, ayurvedic drugs, petrochemicals and oils, etc.

We are supported by our technical expertise and sound infrastructure. We offer analysis and testing solutions on a wide range of products and materials since last four decades. Our expert and specialized services have helped a large number of our customers in optimizing their business operations.

These services are delivered by our team of highly trained professionals as per (I.P, B.P, E.P, Ayurvedic Pharmacopeia, U.S.P, CIPAC, IS specifications etc.) or as per the customers specifications.
